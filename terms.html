NIKE TERMS AND CONDITION

Confidentiality

Nike Product testing must be kept a secret. Applicant must be discreet in carrying out surveys without raising a dust in order to achieve organic result. Keeping Nike product testing a secret is to maintain the integrity of the testing process and prevent leaks of confidential information about the products being tested. It helps Nike control the release of new products and surprises for our customers. If you are selected for product testing, remember to keep it hush-hush until Nike gives the go ahead to share any information.

Punctuality

Applicant must be punctual and diligent to surveys, survey tasks and compiling of feedbacks. Bonuses are time-based.

Mobility

Applicants must be duly mobile and not suffer any ailment limiting general body function and movement of limbs.

Proactiveness

Applicant must be articulate in surveying, counting , writing , and describing tested products and services rendered by our staffs.
